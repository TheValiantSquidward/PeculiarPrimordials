Foreyia maxkuhni is a species of strange parrotfish-like coelecanth from the middle triassic period. It has a very unusual name, but that's prehistoric creatures foreyia!
<NEWLINE>
Foreyia loves crushing corals into a fine sand with its teeth. you can use this to an advantage by feeding it coral, allowing you an unlimited supply of sand.
